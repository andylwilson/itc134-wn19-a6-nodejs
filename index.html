Introduction

Hello and welcome to the project website for our ITC134 class project, created by Andy, Elizabeth, Karolyn, and Mona. Thanks for stopping by!

Our goal

Our goal with this website is to present a quick and easy to absorb exploration of the basics of Node.js, by giving you a brief history of the language, telling you why you might want to learn and use Node.js, and finally presenting a step-by-step tutorial of how to run your very first simple "Hello World" app using Node.js on Cloud9.
